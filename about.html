ABOUT OUR PRINT HOUSE

Built On Craft, Scaled By Technology

Since day one, Eth-Cana Printing PLC has focused on one goal: deliver premium print quality with dependable turnaround times for fast-moving businesses.

OUR JOURNEY

Milestones Across the Years

2000

Foundation and Core Print Services

Established as a dedicated press focused on essential jobs such as BC, LH, cards, and flyer production.

2003

Machine Capacity Expansion

Added additional offset and digital lines to take on larger brochure, magazine, and book print orders.

2007

Finishing and Fulfillment Growth

Expanded into post-press finishing, binding, and coordinated delivery for complete end-to-end print jobs.

2015

Specialized Product Lines

Introduced focused production for labels, paper stickers, vouchers, posters, calendars, and agendas.

2020

Modern Prepress and Quality Workflow

Implemented digital proofing and stronger prepress controls to reduce rework and speed up final delivery timelines.

SERVICES IN DETAIL

How We Deliver Each Service

Business Essentials Printing

Built for everyday business identity and stationery needs with clean typography, consistent brand tone, and reliable paper quality.

Includes BC, LH, Digital ID, and Cards with press-ready color control and precision trimming.

Publishing & Editorial Printing

Long-run and short-run printing for content-heavy materials with balanced ink coverage and durable binding options.

Includes Books, Magazines, Brochures, and Flyers for education, corporate, and campaign distribution.

Brand & Promotion Materials

Built for visibility-focused campaigns where color strength and clean finishing directly impact brand perception.

Includes Posters, Paper Sticker, Labels, and Vouchers for product labeling, promotions, and customer engagement.

Calendar & Agenda Printing

Structured layout printing for annual planning documents used in schools, offices, and administrative workflows.

Includes Calendar and Agenda formats with strong paper options and finishing styles suited for daily use.
